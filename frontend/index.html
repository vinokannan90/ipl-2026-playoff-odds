How this works

  • Format: 10 teams, 14 league matches each, 70 total. Win = 2 pts, No Result = 1 pt, Loss = 0.
  • Playoffs: Top 4 qualify (Q1, Eliminator, Q2, Final).
  • Tiebreakers: Wins → NRR → H2H wins (completed + in-sim) → random share.
  • Data: Standings/schedule from scores.iplt20.com via our cached backend proxy. We never fabricate scores or NRR.
  • Honesty: 50/50 default has zero predictive signal. LLM priors and your bias slider are the levers.
