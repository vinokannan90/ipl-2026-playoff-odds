Live Standings & Playoff Odds

Probabilities are computed by simulating every remaining match. Each match is treated as a 50/50 coin flip unless you set a team bias below. After each simulated full season, the top 4 by (Points → Wins → NRR) qualify. NRR is held at the current real value during simulation — we do not invent run margins. If the simulator finds a tie that would in reality be broken by a margin we cannot know, those teams share the qualifying slot equally.

How this works · Rules encoded

  • Format: 10 teams, 14 league matches each, 70 league matches total. Win = 2 pts, No Result = 1 pt, Loss = 0.
  • Playoffs: Top 4 on the league table qualify (Qualifier 1, Eliminator, Qualifier 2, Final).
  • Tiebreakers: (1) more wins, (2) higher NRR, (3) head-to-head wins (from completed matches), (4) random share if still tied.
  • Data sources (live, JSONP): 284-groupstandings.js for the table, 284-matchschedule.js for fixtures (competition 284 = IPL 2026). We never fabricate scores or NRR — only what the official feed reports.
  • Honest limitations: 50/50 default contains zero predictive signal — it just maps the schedule to outcome distributions. Use the per-team bias slider to inject your own prior.
